About us

Sino-Zam VCST is a leading training instituition located in the town of
Luanshya on the Copperbelt Province
We strive each day to train our students with appropriate skills accurately, safely
in order to safe guard our college's reputation as a leading instituition in high practical
training and to foster the success of our students in their area of speciality.

Mission

To provide skills that meet the needs of the industry and national demand


Vision

To be the college of Excellence and the main base of high quality technical skills and
entreprenuership training in Zambia and beyond.


Goal

To provide the best possible learning environment and opportunities for all.
